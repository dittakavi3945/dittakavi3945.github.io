01 — About

Engineering meets

I'm Avinash, a Mechanical Design Engineer currently based in Liverpool, UK. I hold an MSc in Mechanical Engineering Design with Management from the University of Liverpool and have hands-on experience across renewable energy, automotive, and manufacturing sectors.

My design philosophy is simple: understand the constraints, work within them brilliantly. Whether that's sizing heat pump systems for MCS compliance, reverse engineering a torch in PTC Creo, or reducing material costs through smarter component design — I bring analytical rigour and practical creativity to every project.

I believe technology changes fast, but the principles of good design — rooted in understanding people — remain constant.
